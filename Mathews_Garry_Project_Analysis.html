Site Intent

The site intent for Exotic Animal Adventure in general is to inform and attract potential clients. The site will also provide items for sale, have a gallery, inform of upcoming events and provide information on some of the more popular animals. The site redesign will include the following:

  • Validation of all code, ensuring W3C Compliance
  • A visually appealing & focused design
  • An upgraded color pallet more suited to the park theme
  • A CTA to request information on birthday parties

Target Audience

Most of the demographics for this type of business or site show a very wide range but a few groups do stand out.The primary target audience for Exotic Animal Experience are going to be Young to middle aged adults with children or grand children, with the largest group falling in the 35-44 age range. They will have an annual income of less that $150k the largest group being in the $50-100k range. Education levels vary widely with the largest percentage having a college education.
